Top Ten Must See Shows

#1 Game Of Thrones



Series: Game Of Thrones
Network: HBO
A very addictive show that includes dragons, kings, and queens and always grabs your attention.

#2 The Walking Dead



Series: The Walking Dead
Network: AMC
A show based in a post zombie apocalyptic world, very action based, and very good.

#3 WestWorld



Series: WestWorld
Network: HBO
An AI based show where robots become so realistic it’s hard to tell between humans and AI.

#4 Stranger Things



Series: Stranger Things
Network: Netflix
A very interesting show that takes place in the 1980s when a boy gets trapped in a 3rd dimension.

#5 Breaking Bad



Series: Breaking Bad
Network: AMC
A chemistry teacher finds out bad news and turns into a massive meth producer.

#6 13 Reasons Why



Series: 13 Reasons Why
Network: Netflix
A girl commits suicide and leaves behind tapes for each person who caused her death.

#7 The OA



Series: The OA
Network: Netflix
A girl goes missing and comes back with her vision, the story it tells keeps you guessing.

#8 The 100


Series: The 100
Network: The CW
Post-apocalyptic show where 100 kids get sent back to nuclear waste covered earth.

#9 Blue Mountain State



Series: Blue Mountain State
Network: Spike
A hilarious show based on football and partying life in college following the GOAT house.

#10 Supernatural



Series: Supernatural
Network: The CW
A series following the Winchester brothers who chase and kill supernatural things.
